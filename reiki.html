REIKI

Reiki, a form of energy healing, is believed to offer a wide range of benefits for physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual well-being. While scientific research on Reiki is still evolving, many practitioners and individuals report experiencing the following benefits:
Stress Reduction
Pain Relief
Improved Sleep
Enhanced Emotional Well-being
Boosted Immune System
Increased Energy Levels
Spiritual Growth and Awareness
Support for Healing Trauma
Complementary Therapy for Various Conditions
Overall Well-being and Balance
It's important to note that individual experiences with Reiki may vary, while many people find it beneficial, it should not be used as a substitute for professional medical treatment when necessary. It's always advisable to consult with a qualified Reiki practitioner and healthcare provider to determine the most appropriate approach for individual health and needs.
